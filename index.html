Approach

Simple Data Science?

My idea is to make data science applicable and easy to understand. It doesn’t have to be rocket science. Nowadays, there are great libraries available that let us apply machine learning on many problems quick and easy, thus createing some real value for any company - and that’s what I like to do. Simple data science. No overengineering.

What About That Hype?

And let’s be honest: In some cases, Machine Learning or AI is not (yet) the right solution for a problem. Maybe a simple tableau dashboard that makes data from Access or Excel more transparent for everyone is the right way to go. That’s totally fine - and part of my work approach: Let’s ignore all that buzz and keep our feet on the ground.
